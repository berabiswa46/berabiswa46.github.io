## About MIRAI VISION LAB

### Our Vision

At MIRAI VISION LAB, we envision a future where artificial intelligence and computer vision technologies seamlessly integrate into society, enhancing human capabilities and addressing critical challenges across healthcare, transportation, manufacturing, and beyond. We strive to be at the forefront of research that not only advances scientific knowledge but also creates meaningful real-world impact.

### Our Mission

Our mission is threefold:

1. **Pioneering Research**: Conduct cutting-edge research in computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence that pushes the boundaries of what's possible
2. **Education & Training**: Nurture the next generation of researchers and engineers through rigorous academic training and hands-on research experience
3. **Innovation & Impact**: Translate research discoveries into practical solutions that benefit society and industry

## Facilities & Equipment

### Computing Infrastructure
- **GPU Cluster**: 16x NVIDIA A100 GPUs for deep learning training
- **High-Performance Workstations**: 20+ workstations with RTX 4090 GPUs
- **Storage**: 500TB network-attached storage for datasets
- **Cloud Computing**: Access to AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure platforms

### Laboratory Equipment
- **Cameras & Sensors**: High-resolution cameras, depth sensors, LiDAR, thermal cameras
- **Robotic Platforms**: Mobile robots for vision-based navigation research
- **Medical Imaging Equipment**: Mock diagnostic systems for algorithm testing
- **VR/AR Devices**: Equipment for augmented and virtual reality research

### Software & Tools
- Deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX)
- Computer vision libraries (OpenCV, Detectron2, MMDetection)
- 3D processing tools (Open3D, PCL)
- Development platforms (Docker, Kubernetes, MLflow)

### Collaboration Spaces
- **Main Lab Space**: 2000 sq ft open research area
- **Meeting Rooms**: Multiple conference rooms with video conferencing
- **Presentation Theater**: 50-seat auditorium for seminars and talks
- **Break Area**: Informal collaboration and discussion space
