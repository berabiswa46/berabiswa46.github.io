MIRAI VISION LAB is excited to announce a strategic partnership with TechVision Corp, a leading technology company in computer vision solutions for industrial automation.

***

**Partnership Details**:

This collaboration will focus on developing next-generation computer vision systems for manufacturing and quality control applications. The partnership includes:

- **Research Funding**: $500,000 over 2 years
- **Technology Transfer**: Joint development of industrial-grade vision systems
- **Student Opportunities**: Internships and co-op positions for our graduate students
- **Equipment Access**: State-of-the-art cameras and sensors for research

**Research Focus Areas**:
1. Real-time defect detection in manufacturing
2. Robotic vision for automated assembly
3. Quality inspection using deep learning
4. 3D vision for bin picking applications

**Impact**: This partnership bridges the gap between academic research and real-world industrial applications, ensuring our innovations reach the market and create practical impact.

We look forward to a productive collaboration that benefits both academia and industry!
